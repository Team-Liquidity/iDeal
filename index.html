Why customers choose store?

We are offering countless collection with affordable prices for the local stores craft products, to encourage our local community charities to sell their products online. Since many of them do not have the capital to start their own websites, in this way many charities will be able to display their products online.

Can you sell with us?

Of course, you can sell your products online through our website. If you have craft products and want to sell your products through us, please visit the sell with us page, where we will ask you for some information to fill out with the form, and then the product will be displayed with the details on the products page.

who are we?

We are software developers who decided to use our programming skills to help charities in our community to market their local goods through the Internet, which will help them find a stable source of income to develop their community businesses. If you are curious to know us personally you can visit our team page.
